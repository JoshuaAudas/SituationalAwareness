Situational Awareness

Situational awareness is very important in your day to day life. Being aware of your surroundings can help you identify what is around you and how it can be used to protect you in a dangerous situation. People can be unpredictable and dangerous, so learning how to use your situational awareness to its fullest extent is something that everyone should learn how to do.
